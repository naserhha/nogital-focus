Frequently Asked Questions

How does the website blocker work?
Our browser extension blocks distracting websites during your focus sessions. You can whitelist essential sites and set custom schedules for different days.
Can I use NoGital Focus on multiple devices?
Yes! Your account syncs across all your devices. Start a focus session on your computer and continue on your phone seamlessly.
What if I need to pause my focus session?
You can pause and resume sessions anytime. We understand that emergencies happen, and your focus data will still be tracked accurately.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal data. Your focus habits are private and secure.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
